Cophall parking FAQs

Where is Cophall Farm located?+

Cophall Farm is located on Copthorne Road, just a few minutes' drive from both Gatwick terminals. The car park is clearly signposted from the main road.

How often do the shuttles run?+

Shuttle buses depart every 10-15 minutes during peak times, with journey times of around 5-10 minutes to the terminal.

Is the car park secure?+

Yes. The site has 24-hour security, CCTV coverage, and perimeter fencing. Staff are on-site around the clock.

Can I book for both terminals?+

Absolutely. Cophall Farm serves both the North and South terminals at Gatwick. The shuttle service covers both terminals on every run.

Why choose Cophall Farm?

Cophall Farm has been providing Gatwick airport parking for many years, building a strong reputation for reliability and value. Their well-maintained site offers tarmac parking with clear markings and easy access.

With regular shuttle services running throughout the day and night, you can be confident of reaching your terminal on time. The friendly staff are available around the clock to assist with any queries, and the site benefits from comprehensive security measures including 24-hour patrols and full CCTV coverage.
