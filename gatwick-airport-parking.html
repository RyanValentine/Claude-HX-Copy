Gatwick parking FAQs

How far in advance should I book?+

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks before your trip for the best prices. Prices increase closer to your travel date as availability decreases.

Can I cancel my booking?+

Yes! All Holiday Extras bookings come with free cancellation up to the day before your booking starts. No questions asked.

Is my car insured while parked?+

All of our approved car parks have 24-hour security and CCTV. Your car is covered by the car park's insurance while on site.

How do transfers work for off-site parking?+

Shuttle buses run regularly between the car park and both Gatwick terminals. Journey times are typically 5-15 minutes depending on the car park.
