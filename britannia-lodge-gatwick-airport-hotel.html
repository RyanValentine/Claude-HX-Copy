About Britannia Lodge

The Britannia Lodge is a 3-star hotel near Gatwick airport, ideal for travellers looking for affordable, comfortable accommodation before an early flight. The hotel offers shuttle transfers to both terminals and is a popular choice for budget-conscious holidaymakers.

Rooms are clean, comfortable, and well-equipped with en-suite bathrooms, complimentary WiFi, and tea and coffee making facilities. The friendly reception team is available around the clock to help with any queries.

Britannia Lodge FAQs

How far is the hotel from Gatwick airport?+

The Britannia Lodge is a short shuttle ride from both Gatwick terminals. The shuttle runs regularly and journey times are approximately 10 minutes.

Is parking included?+

Parking is available as an add-on. You can book a room-only stay or choose one of our hotel and parking packages which include up to 15 days of secure parking.

Is breakfast available?+

Yes, breakfast is available at the hotel. You can choose to add breakfast when you book, or purchase it on the morning of your stay at reception.

What time can I check in and check out?+

Check-in is available from 2pm onwards. Check-out is by 10am. If you have an early flight, the shuttle service starts running from early morning to get you to the terminal on time.
