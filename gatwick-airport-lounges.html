Lounge FAQs

How long can I stay in the lounge?+

Most lounges allow you to enjoy the space for up to 3 hours before your flight. Some premium lounges offer longer stays.

Can children use the lounge?+

Yes, children are welcome in all Gatwick lounges. Under-2s are usually free, and there are reduced rates for children aged 2-11.

What's included?+

All lounges include complimentary drinks, snacks, WiFi, and comfortable seating. Premium lounges add hot meals, bar service, and spa facilities.

Do I need to book in advance?+

We recommend booking in advance as lounges can fill up, especially during peak travel periods. Booking through Holiday Extras also guarantees you the best price.

Why book a lounge with Holiday Extras?

Starting your holiday in an airport lounge transforms the travel experience. Instead of busy departure halls, enjoy a calm environment with complimentary refreshments, comfortable seating, and free WiFi. Whether you are travelling for business or leisure, an airport lounge is the perfect way to unwind before your flight.
